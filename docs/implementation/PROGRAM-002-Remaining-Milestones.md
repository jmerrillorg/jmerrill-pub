# PROGRAM-002 Remaining Milestones

Program: PROGRAM-002 - Autonomous Publishing Production Pipeline

Status: Remaining major milestones after Phase I

## Certified Baseline

Phase I is certified through:

- Active Author Workspace
- Production Pipeline visibility
- Commissioning Hold

The remaining work begins after this point.

## Remaining Major Milestones

### Distribution Release

Move from Commissioning Hold to controlled release execution.

Major outcomes:

- final release approval
- channel/file/metadata readiness
- release action evidence
- no accidental publication before approval

### Royalty Automation

Prepare reporting and payment readiness after release/reportable activity.

Major outcomes:

- royalty source confirmation
- statement generation/readiness
- payment gate
- author-facing royalty dashboard maturation

### Post-Publication Author Success

Support the author relationship after release.

Major outcomes:

- support cadence
- catalog health
- post-release issue handling
- future title pathway

### Catalog Adoption / OP-000

Bring legacy, active, and published titles into PROGRAM-002 without lifecycle restart.

Major outcomes:

- state certification
- workspace linking
- historical evidence
- resume-at-current-gate behavior

### Legacy Author Workspace

Expose safe workspace surfaces for authors whose titles predate PROGRAM-002.

Major outcomes:

- identity/title matching
- legacy evidence display
- active/inactive relationship handling

### Returning Author Experience

Support new projects for existing authors without duplicate portals.

Major outcomes:

- existing author portal reuse
- additional title creation
- update-only onboarding
- per-title agreement/payment/release path

## Not Milestones

The following are implementation tasks, not major milestones:

- copy edits
- individual field labels
- small route fixes
- isolated validation commands
- one-off documentation syncs
- stale branch cleanup

They should be handled under the appropriate milestone or incidental completion rule.

## Explicitly Not Authorized by Phase I Closeout

- public launch/release
- retailer submission
- Ingram submission
- preorder
- royalty payment
- author payment
- Business Central posting
- QBO usage
- duplicate author portal creation
- duplicate workspace creation
